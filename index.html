about

Safe Finance was founded in 2001, shortly before the Great Global Crisis occurred. The company was founded by Amit Elgabasi, who after decades of working in international markets saw how the capital market was changing before his eyes and made a big change to the field of finance and economic management.

the Director:

amit Elgabasi, the director of Safe Finance Company, has a bachelor's degree in economics and management and was previously a director of the Safe Insurance Company.

the assistant Director:

The company's assistant Director Betty Binyamin has a bachelor's degree in industry and management and specializes in risk management and financial consulting.

the team:

As part of the professional team there are several professionals: risk management consultants, investment consultants and more.

info-Insurance:

The company provides insurance services in all areas of our daily life such as: car insurance, life insurance, home insurance and more. In addition, the company gives the option of canceling the customer's deductible and assumes the participation fee.

info-investments:

The company provides investment services to the client, and guides him through every step of his investment planning, starting from an explanation of the investment field to the moment of the investment itself. You can invest in many areas such as: the food sector, the automotive sector and the natural resources sector.

info-loans:

The company gives the possibility to take a loan for any need, and offers its customers a loan at a low interest rate and in a layout for the customer's convenience.
